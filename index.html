About Me


Hi there! I'm Kaushik, a software quality engineer based in Hyderabad. With 4 years of experience in software industry, I'm dedicated to ensuring the quality and reliability of software systems.


My expertise lies in manual and automated testing methodologies including API testing, performance testing along with defect tracking. I have a strong background in designing and executing comprehensive API test suites, as well as analyzing and reporting test results.


Through my work, I aim to contribute to the development of roboust and user-friendly software products. I believe that thorough testing plays a vital role in delivering high-quality solutions to end-users.


One of the reasons I have created this GitHub page is to share my knowledge and experiences in software testing. Over the years, I have learned valuable lessons, discovered effective techniques, and encountered various challenges in the testing domain. I believe in the power of knowledge sharing and want to contribute to the growth of the software testing community by providing insights, best practices, and practical tips through this platform.


When I'm not diving into test cases and bug reports, you can find me exploring the advancements in software testing tools and techniques.
